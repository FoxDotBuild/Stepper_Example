# Stepper

Raw control of a stepper board on Arduino.

|Arduino     |Board |
|------------|------|
|PIN         |PIN   |
|9           |IN1   |
|8           |IN2   |
|10          |IN3   |
|11          |IN4   |
|+5          |+     |
|GND         |GND   |

# Dimensions

28mm motor body diameter
35 mm between the screw holes
4 mm screw hole
20 mm motor body height
5 mm diameter shaft
3 mm across the dual flats
6 mm flat length on shaft
